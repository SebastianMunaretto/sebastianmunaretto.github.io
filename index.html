3D Reconstruction with NeRF, SfM, MVS, and SLAM

Sebastian Munaretto · Free University of Bozen-Bolzano · 2025

This thesis explores real-world 3D reconstruction pipelines using Structure from Motion, Multi-View Stereo, Neural Radiance Fields, and SLAM. The work includes data collection with Meta Aria Glasses and evaluation of frameworks such as COLMAP, Nerfstudio, and MASt3R-SLAM.

Keywords

SfM, MVS, NeRF, SLAM, computer vision, 3D reconstruction, Meta Aria, COLMAP, Nerfstudio

Summary

The project compares modern 3D reconstruction approaches in practical environments, with a focus on quality, robustness, and applicability to real-world data.
